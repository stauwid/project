Welcome to Stauw ID. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our application.

By using the Stauw ID, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or directly provide personal information to use our basic services.

1.2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use our application, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, IP address, mobile network information, and hardware specifications.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous data about how you interact with our app, such as which features you use, frequency and duration of use, music playback patterns, and app launch count.
  • Error Logs: Information to help identify and troubleshoot app crashes and technical issues.
  • Advertising ID: The app collects Google's Advertising ID (GAID) to serve personalized ads when you consent to this feature.

2. Permissions We Request

Our app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Internet Access: Required to stream music, display advertisements, and send anonymous analytical data.
  • Notification Access: Allows us to display music player controls in your notification area while playing music.
  • Foreground Service: Enables music playback when the app is in the background.
  • Advertising ID Access: Allows us to serve personalized advertisements based on your preferences and comply with advertising regulations.

You can manage these permissions through your device settings, although restricting certain permissions may limit app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide and Maintain Services: To deliver the core functionality of our music streaming app and make improvements based on usage patterns.
  • Personalize User Experience: To understand how you use our app and optimize its features for better usability.
  • Display Advertisements: To show relevant advertisements that help support our free app and services. This includes personalized ads when you consent to them.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance, content, and features.
  • Service Management: To manage the frequency and timing of advertisements to enhance user experience.
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries or service-related issues.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations, including those related to privacy and data protection.

4. Advertising

4.1. Ad Services

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob and related Google services. These services may use technologies such as cookies, device identifiers, and tracking pixels to collect information about your app usage for the purpose of delivering relevant ads.

4.2. Ad Types and Frequency

The app displays several types of advertisements:

  • App Open Ads: These may appear when you launch the app after a period of inactivity.
  • Banner Ads: These appear in designated areas within the app interface.
  • Rewarded Interstitial Ads: These full-screen ads may appear after playing multiple songs and offer rewards for viewing.

We implement measures to ensure that ad frequency is reasonable and does not significantly impact your experience.

4.3. Ad Personalization

During the initial app setup, you can choose whether to allow personalized advertisements based on your interests and app usage. You can change this preference at any time in the app settings.

4.4. Third-party Ad Networks

For information about how Google uses data for advertising purposes, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms and Google's Privacy Policy.
